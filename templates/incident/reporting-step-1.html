A Quick Note Before You Report An Incident

For this site to succeed, it needs to focus on its core mission: the identification of aggressive motorists that seem to be harrasing cyclists intentionally or endangering them out of sheer ignorance -- some drivers simply do not realize that passing a cyclists with a foot or less of clearance endangers the cyclist.

There is no point in reporting every three foot violation you experience, but please report the ones that were especially dangerous, especially if your encounter was intentional and you believe the action may be repeated or visited upon other cyclists.

Please read the directions on the next page carefully, I manage this project in my "free time" and it saves me a lot of work when the directions are followed. It often creates time consuming work when they are not. I thank you for your consideration in advance. Do NOT identify a driver in your report. If you have that information you can send it to me in an email.
