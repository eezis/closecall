Privacy Statement

I wanted to take a quick moment to post this privacy statement because some people are spooked by Strava's default authorization page which rather than stating what an application does cites, what an application may do including; "Access your activity data" and "Display public data related to those activities." This site does not do that.

It simply uses Strava's authorization service to create your Close Call Database account quickly and efficiently.

If future services are added to this site that require more data, it would only happen with your permission, on an opt-in basis. You can also revoke access to this application in your Strava settings.

TLDR; you know all that stupid [lack-of] privacy stuff that Facebook does that makes you angry? This site doesn't do any of that and never will.
